Binesh Favourite Recipe

Aloo Gopi Dry

Aloo Gobi is a classic Indian dish featuring potatoes and cauliflower cooked with a flavorful blend of spices. This recipe offers a simple and delicious way to prepare this popular vegetarian dish. The key to a good Aloo Gobi lies in properly sautéing the vegetables and allowing the spices to bloom, creating a rich and aromatic curry. It's a versatile dish that can be enjoyed with roti, paratha, rice, or naan, making it a perfect addition to any meal.

Spicy Kadai Chickpea (or Rajma/Peas) is a flavorful Indian dish featuring chickpeas or your choice of legumes cooked with a vibrant blend of spices. This recipe provides an easy and delicious way to prepare this popular vegetarian dish. The key to a good Spicy Kadai Chickpea lies in properly sautéing the onions and allowing the spices to release their aromatic essence, creating a rich and satisfying meal. It's a versatile dish that can be enjoyed with roti, paratha, rice, or naan, making it a perfect addition to any meal.

Peanut Chutney

Peanut Chutney is a classic South Indian condiment featuring roasted peanuts blended with a flavorful mix of spices. This recipe offers a simple and delicious way to prepare this popular accompaniment. The key to a good Peanut Chutney lies in properly roasting the peanuts and allowing the spices to bloom, creating a rich and aromatic paste. It's a versatile chutney that can be enjoyed with idli, dosa, vada, or uttapam, making it a perfect addition to any South Indian meal.
